About Saraswati Vidya Academy

Greetings and Welcome to Saraswati Vidya Academy, located in the serene surroundings of Lonavla. Our school is an English medium, coeducational residential institution that caters to academically gifted children from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. SVA provides education from grade 6 onwards, under the CBSE board.
