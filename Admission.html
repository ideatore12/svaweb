Eligibility

Students who meet the following criteria are eligible to apply:

Students currently studying in Class 5 in a government/government-aided school.
AND

Students whose family's annual income should be less than Rs. 3Lakhs for the year.

How to Apply

Aspiring students apply online for admission to Class 6 at www.saraswatividya.academy

Selection Process:

Step 1: Aspiring students apply online for admission to Class 6 at www.saraswatividya.academy

Step 2: Eligible students are invited for the SVA Selection Test (SVAST) which will be held at a centre close to their homes.

Step 3: Meritorious students are interviewed with their parents/guardians.

Step 4: Successful students are offered admission to the Saraswati Vidya Academy.

Last Date of Application:30th june 2023
